The eleven laws

All eleven, every day, for 75 days. Miss one and you restart from Day 1 and forfeit a day's Roadie earnings.

  • 1Fast — water only, sunrise to sunset
  • 2Earn $50+ on Roadie
  • 3Move 30 min — walk or run
  • 4Train — 4 weights / 3 bodyweight
  • 5120–150g protein
  • 6FE exam prep — 30 min
  • 791st Foundation — 20 min
  • 8Log every dollar spent
  • 9Cold shower — 30–60s
  • 10Night close — tomorrow's #1 + today in a line
  • 11Phone guard — none first & last 30 min

Restart the contract?

This sets you back to Day 1 and clears every completed day. There's no half-measure — that's the whole point.

Your progress photos and 91st milestones stay. They're real work and don't reset.
